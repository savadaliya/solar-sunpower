2. PERC Solar Penal

PERC technology to put pressure on traditional solar cells in supply chain

Mono PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) solar panels represent an evolutionary step in PV technology, improving the efficiency of conventional monocrystalline solar cells. Mono PERC panels place a passivation layer on the backside of the cell to aid in absorbing light and minimizing electron recombination, resulting in better energy conversion rates.

Technoloy
Key Features:-
  1. P-Type PERC Cell
  2. Module Efficiency- 20.87%
  3. G12 gallium doped wafer
  4. 12BB half-cell technology
  5. 6 rows design in 132-HC cell layout

In standard monocrystalline solar cells, some sunlight passes through without being absorbed, and certain electrons recombine before contributing to the electric current, reducing efficiency. Mono PERC technology addresses these issues by adding a passivation layer to the rear surface of the cell. This layer serves two primary functions:

  1. Reflecting Unabsorbed Light: The passivation layer reflects light that passes through the silicon cell back into the cell, providing a second chance for photon absorption and electricity generation.
  2. Reducing Electron Recombination: The passivation layer minimizes the recombination of electrons, allowing more free electrons to contribute to the electric current, thereby increasing the cell's efficiency.

  • Higher Efficiency
  • Improved Temperature Coefficient
  • Better Performance in Low-Light Conditions
